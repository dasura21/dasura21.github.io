Mechanical Design & Automation

Autonomous Spring-Powered Sled

Engineered a scale model sled capable of autonomously transporting a 100g load over variable distances. The system is entirely powered by a torsion spring and features a custom-coded self-stopping braking mechanism utilizing real-time positional data.

  • Design Space: Evaluated 1,296 concepts via morphological charts to select the optimal locking mechanism.
  • Hardware: Arduino, Ultrasonic Sensor, Servo Motor, Torsion Spring.
  • Fabrication: SolidWorks CAD modeling and 3D-printed PLA locking case.
